Nairobi

Fri 14 September 2001
By Calle Rundgren

In misc.

tags: KenyaNairobiTraffic

I am having a nice stay at PrideInn Lantana, Nairobi. Have been very busy days since I arrived here, mostly on the founding of the travels inside the city takes pretty long time.

There is kind of interesting how much traffic there is here, but still the condition of the roads is really bad. Maybe that's how it is in development countrys, idk. I would most certainly never sit down in the drivers seat here.

I ate my first Kenyan meal ever today, we had lunch catered to teh office, I'm not very pleased. But I guess it tastes better in the mouths of them who eat meat.

Tomorrow it is time for Uganda. It's gonna be fun to stay in Kampala for two days before moving on to Mbarara.

Nigthy.
